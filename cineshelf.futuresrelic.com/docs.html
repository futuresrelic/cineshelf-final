Welcome to CineShelf

Your personal movie collection manager

CineShelf is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to help you manage your physical movie collection with ease. Whether you collect DVDs, Blu-rays, or 4K UHDs, CineShelf provides powerful tools to organize, track, and share your collection.

Current Version: v2.2.1 - Production ready with all features stable

What is CineShelf?

CineShelf combines personal collection management with social features, creating a comprehensive platform for movie enthusiasts. It's perfect for collectors who want to:

  • Track their physical movie collection (DVDs, Blu-rays, 4K UHDs)
  • Maintain a wishlist of movies they want to acquire
  • Share collections with family and friends
  • Avoid buying duplicates when shopping
  • Test their movie knowledge with trivia games
  • Keep track of borrowed movies

Technology

CineShelf is built with modern web technologies:

  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks), HTML5, CSS3
  • Backend: PHP 7.4+ with SQLite database
  • Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0

Quick Start Guide

Get up and running in 5 minutes

Step 1: Sign In

  1. Navigate to CineShelf in your web browser
  2. Click "Sign in with Google"
  3. Authorize the application with your Google account
First Time? A new account will be created automatically using your Google profile.

Step 2: Add Your First Movie

  1. Go to the Collection tab
  2. Click the "Add Movie" button
  3. Search for a movie by title
  4. Select the correct movie from search results
  5. Fill in details:
    • Format: DVD, Blu-ray, 4K UHD, etc.
    • Edition: Standard, Director's Cut, Collector's Edition, etc.
    • Condition: Mint, Excellent, Good, Fair
    • Location: Where you store it (optional)
  6. Click "Add to Collection"

Step 3: Explore Features

  • Filter your collection by format, genre, rating, year, or director
  • Add movies to wishlist from the Wishlist tab
  • Create a group to share with family in the Groups tab
  • Play trivia to test your movie knowledge in the Game tab

Step 4: Install as an App (Optional)

CineShelf works great in your browser, but you can also install it as a standalone app:

On Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

  1. Click the install icon in the address bar (or three-dot menu)
  2. Click "Install"

On Mobile (iOS/Android)

  1. iOS: Tap Share ‚Üí "Add to Home Screen"
  2. Android: Tap menu ‚Üí "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

Collection Management

Track and organize your physical movie collection

Overview

The Collection feature is the heart of CineShelf, allowing you to catalog every movie you own with detailed information about format, edition, condition, and more.

Adding Movies

There are several ways to add movies to your collection:

Method 1: Search by Title

  1. Click "Add Movie"
  2. Type the movie title in the search box
  3. Select from search results powered by TMDB
  4. Fill in copy details and save

Method 2: Barcode Scanning

  1. Click the barcode icon in the Add Movie dialog
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Point camera at the barcode on your movie case
  4. CineShelf will automatically search and add the movie

Method 3: Manual Entry

For rare or obscure titles not in TMDB:

  1. Search for the title
  2. Click "Can't find it? Add manually"
  3. Enter title, year, and other details manually

Wishlist

Keep track of movies you want to add to your collection

What is the Wishlist?

The Wishlist lets you maintain a list of movies you want to buy. It's perfect for:

  • Planning future purchases
  • Tracking upcoming releases
  • Sharing gift ideas with family
  • Price comparison shopping

Adding to Wishlist

  1. Go to the Wishlist tab
  2. Click "Add to Wishlist"
  3. Search for the movie
  4. Set wishlist details:
    • Priority: High, Medium, or Low
    • Target Format: Preferred format to buy
    • Target Edition: Specific edition you want
    • Max Price: Most you're willing to pay
    • Notes: Additional info (e.g., "Wait for sale")
  5. Click "Add to Wishlist"

Priority Levels

  • High Priority: Movies you really want, highest on shopping list
  • Medium Priority: Interested but can wait
  • Low Priority: Nice to have eventually

Quick Add to Collection

When you acquire a wishlist movie:

  1. Find the movie in your wishlist
  2. Click "Add to Collection"
  3. The target format and edition pre-fill automatically
  4. Complete any additional details
  5. Save - the movie moves to your collection and removes from wishlist

Group Wishlist

When you're in a group, you can view everyone's wishlists together:

  1. Go to Groups tab
  2. Select a group
  3. Click "Group Wishlist"
  4. See all members' wanted movies in one place

This is perfect for:

  • Avoiding duplicate gifts
  • Birthday and holiday shopping
  • Coordinating group buys

Groups & Sharing

Share your collection with family and friends

What are Groups?

Groups allow you to share your movie collection with family and friends. Perfect for:

  • Families who share a physical collection
  • Movie enthusiasts comparing collections
  • Avoiding duplicate purchases in households
  • Tracking borrowed movies

Creating a Group

  1. Go to the Groups tab
  2. Click "Create Group"
  3. Enter group details:
    • Name: Group name (e.g., "Smith Family Collection")
    • Description: Optional description
  4. Click "Create"
You're the Admin: As the creator, you have admin privileges to manage members and settings.

Inviting Members

  1. Open your group
  2. Click "Invite Member"
  3. Enter the person's email address
  4. An invite link will be generated
  5. Share the invite link with the person (via email, text, etc.)

When they click the link:

  • If they have a CineShelf account: They join immediately
  • If they're new: They'll be prompted to create an account first

Architecture Overview

Technical design and structure

System Architecture

CineShelf uses a modern web application architecture:

Frontend

  • Pure JavaScript - No frameworks, ~5,500 lines of code
  • Module Pattern - Organized into logical modules with IIFEs
  • Responsive Design - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • PWA - Service worker for offline support

Backend

  • PHP 7.4+ - Server-side logic
  • SQLite 3 - Database with WAL mode
  • RESTful API - Single endpoint, action-based routing
  • Session Auth - 30-day token lifetime

External Services

  • TMDB API - Movie metadata, posters, ratings
  • Google OAuth - User authentication

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is CineShelf free to use?

A: Yes, CineShelf is completely free and open source.

Q: Does it work offline?

A: Yes! Once loaded, CineShelf works offline thanks to service worker caching. You can browse your collection without internet.

Q: Can I track digital movies?

A: Yes, "Digital" is one of the format options. However, CineShelf is designed primarily for physical media.

Q: How many movies can I add?

A: There's no limit. SQLite can handle tens of thousands of movies easily.

Q: Can I add TV shows?

A: Currently CineShelf is focused on movies, but the search includes TV shows if you want to track box sets.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes. Your collection is private by default. Only people in groups you join can see your collection.

Q: Can I export my data?

A: Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data to download your collection as a CSV file.

Q: What if a movie isn't in TMDB?

A: You can add it manually without TMDB metadata. Just search, then click "Can't find it? Add manually".

Q: Can I have multiple formats of the same movie?

A: Yes! You can add multiple copies of the same movie (e.g., DVD + Blu-ray + 4K UHD).

Q: Do group members need to create an account?

A: Yes, they need a free CineShelf account to join groups and view shared collections.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Problem: Can't sign in with Google

Solutions:

  • Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Check that third-party cookies are enabled
  • Try clearing browser cache and cookies
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily

Problem: Movies not loading

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  • Clear browser cache
  • Check browser console for errors (F12)

Problem: Barcode scanner not working

Solutions:

  • Make sure you granted camera permissions
  • Use HTTPS (barcode scanner requires secure connection)
  • Try cleaning the barcode on your movie case
  • Ensure good lighting when scanning
  • On iPhone: Use Safari (Chrome iOS doesn't support camera API)

Problem: Posters not showing

Solutions:

  • Check internet connection (posters load from TMDB)
  • Some older movies may not have posters in TMDB
  • A placeholder will show if poster is unavailable

Problem: App is slow

Solutions:

  • Close other browser tabs
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try list view instead of grid view for large collections
  • Use filters to narrow down results

Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered here:

  • Check browser console (F12) for error messages
  • Try a different browser to isolate the issue
  • Contact your system administrator if self-hosted
