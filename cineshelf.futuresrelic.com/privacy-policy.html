1. Introduction

Welcome to CineShelf ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CineShelf application.

CineShelf is a local-first movie collection manager. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from Google
  • Movie Collection Data: Titles, ratings, notes, and metadata about movies you add to your collection
  • User Preferences: App settings, sort preferences, and display options

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Authentication Tokens: Secure session tokens to keep you logged in
  • Server Database: Your movie collection is stored in our secure SQLite database to enable sync across devices

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • The Movie Database (TMDB): We use TMDB API to fetch movie information (titles, posters, descriptions). TMDB has its own privacy policy: https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain your movie collection
  • Enable multi-user and group collection features
  • Sync your collection across devices
  • Display movie information from TMDB
  • Remember your app preferences and settings

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Server Storage: Your collection is stored on our secure server (cineshelf.futuresrelic.com)
  • Local Storage: Preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • No Encryption: Data is stored in plaintext SQLite database. Do not store sensitive information in movie notes
  • No Backups Provided: You are responsible for exporting your collection if you need backups

6. Camera Permission (Android App Only)

If you use the "Scan Cover" feature, the app requests camera access to:

  • Take a photo of a movie cover
  • Send the image to AI for identification
  • Suggest matching movies from your collection

Images are processed temporarily and not stored permanently.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • TMDB API: Movie search queries are sent to TMDB to retrieve movie information
  • Group Collections: If you create or join a group, other group members can see movies you add to that shared collection

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all your collection data in the app
  • Delete Your Data: Delete individual movies or clear your entire collection
  • Export Your Data: Contact us to request a data export
  • Switch Users: Create multiple user profiles, each with isolated data

9. Children's Privacy

CineShelf does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences to manage movie collections.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Data Retention

  • Your collection data is retained until you manually delete it
  • Inactive accounts are not automatically deleted
  • To delete all your data, contact us at the email below
